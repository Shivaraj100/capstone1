Our Mission

At our affordable computer parts store, we believe that everyone should have access to high-quality computer parts at affordable prices. We are dedicated to providing our customers with a wide selection of computer parts, including GPUs and CPUs, that are both reliable and affordable. We understand that building or upgrading a computer can be a daunting task, which is why we strive to make the process as easy and affordable as possible for our customers.

Our History

Our store was founded in 2023 by a group of computer enthusiasts from YearUp who were tired of seeing overpriced computer parts on the market. They decided to start their own store that focused on providing affordable computer parts without sacrificing quality. Over the years, we have grown into a leading supplier of affordable computer parts, particularly GPUs and CPUs. We have built a loyal customer base by offering excellent customer service and reliable products.

Our Team

Our team is made up of computer experts who are passionate about providing our customers with the best possible products and service. From our knowledgeable sales staff to our experienced technical support team, we are dedicated to helping our customers find the right computer parts for their needs and ensuring that they have a positive shopping experience.
